Additional Experience

Beyond my formal roles, I have gained valuable experience through personal projects and volunteering.

E-Learning Platform for Ethiopian Students

As part of my passion for education and social impact, I developed an e-learning platform tailored specifically for Ethiopian high school students. The platform offers interactive courses in key subjects, such as mathematics and science, along with practice quizzes that help students prepare for their national exams. One of the major challenges I faced was ensuring the platform could be used in rural areas with limited internet access. To overcome this, I implemented an offline-accessible mode that allowed students to download lessons and quizzes to study without needing a constant internet connection. This project not only improved my technical skills in building web applications but also gave me the opportunity to contribute to the educational sector in Ethiopia.

Global Freelance Marketplace

Another project I worked on was creating a global freelance marketplace designed to connect talented freelancers from developing countries with international clients looking for affordable and high-quality services. The platform allows freelancers to set up profiles, list their services, and bid on projects. I implemented secure payment gateways that support multiple currencies, including the Ethiopian Birr, allowing local freelancers to receive payment easily and securely.
